## Curriculum Structure (BGBl. II Nr. 262/2015, Anlage 1.28)

### Entities
- **Jahrgang**: 5 years (I–V), each about 36-38 Wochenstunden
- **Kompetenzmodul (KM)**: Semester-based competency unit, KM 3 through KM 9 (year 1 and year 5 each treated as single block)
- **Fach**: 9 legally mandated specialized subjects in Abschnitt B, plus general subjects in Abschnitt A
- **Pflichtpraktikum**: 8 weeks before entering V. Jahrgang

### Rules
- KM 3 = II. Jahrgang / 3. Semester; KM 9 = V. Jahrgang / 9.+10. Semester
- School-autonomous adaptations allowed within IV. Abschnitt, but no autonomous focus areas (Schwerpunktsetzungen)
- Business English: B1+ target by end of year 4, B2 by end of year 5 (GER)
- The name "Projektmanagement und Netzwerktechnik" does not appear in the legal curriculum — it is a school-level naming convention at HTL Spengergasse
- Relevance mapping (pending confirmation): PMN likely draws from Betriebstechnik (Projektmanagement parts) and Informatik und Informationssysteme (Netzwerke, ERP parts); possibly also Prozessmanagement
